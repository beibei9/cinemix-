Storyline

Harry Potter, a seemingly ordinary boy, discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard and has been accepted to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Upon arriving at Hogwarts, Harry learns about his magical heritage and the mysterious circumstances surrounding his parents' death. Along with his friends Ron and Hermione, Harry embarks on an adventure to uncover the truth about the Sorcerer's Stone and stop the dark forces that threaten the wizarding world. But will they be able to protect the Stone before it's too late?
