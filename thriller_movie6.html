Storyline

Based on true events, Zodiac follows a San Francisco cartoonist who becomes an amateur detective obsessed with the Zodiac killer. As he and a pair of journalists delve into the case, they uncover a complex web of clues, leading them to the killer, but also to a haunting obsession that threatens their lives and sanity.
