Storyline

On the lush alien world of Pandora, a paraplegic former Marine named Jake Sully is selected to participate in the Avatar Program, which enables him to control a genetically engineered body to interact with the native Na'vi people. As he learns their ways and falls in love with a Na'vi woman, he faces a conflict between following his orders and protecting the world he has come to admire.
