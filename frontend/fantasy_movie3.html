Storyline

Four siblings—Peter, Susan, Edmund, and Lucy—are evacuated to the countryside during World War II. While exploring an old wardrobe, Lucy discovers a magical land called Narnia, which is ruled by the evil White Witch. The siblings join forces with Aslan, a great lion, to free Narnia from the witch's spell and restore peace to the land.
