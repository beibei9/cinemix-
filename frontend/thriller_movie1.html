Storyline

Detective William Somerset is a veteran criminal detective who dreams of retiring and moving away from the city and its sinful inhabitants. 7 days before retirement, Somerset faces two troubles: his young partner Mills and a particularly sophisticated murder. The keen mind of an experienced detective immediately determines that this crime is likely to be followed by others. The news confirms his guess. Realizing that the killer is punishing his victims for their mortal sins, the detective is faced with a choice: return to work or leave and hand the case over to his less experienced partner?
